# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 1, 2026**

English GrandMaster ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect and how we use it.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information:

- **Account information**: Email address, password (stored in encrypted form)
- **Profile information**: Display name, country/region
- **Avatar**: Profile image you choose to upload
- **Match data**: Match results, scores, answer history, rating
- **Friends**: Friend code, friend requests, acceptance or rejection records

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- Creating, authenticating, and managing your account
- Providing ranked matches, friend matches, and AI battles
- Calculating and displaying scores, ratings, and rankings
- Providing the friends feature
- Improving the App and addressing technical issues

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following services, which process data on our behalf:

- **Firebase (Google)**: Authentication, database, file storage, cloud functions  
  [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described above or as required by law.

## 4. Data Retention

Your data is stored while your account is active and as required by applicable law. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us through the App settings or the contact information below.

## 5. Security

The App uses Firebase (Google) security features, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data.
